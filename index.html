Monthly Music Hackathon Toronto Edition

At Monthly Music Hackathon, musicians, programmers, artists, scientists, composers, hardware tinkerers, and others spend the day hacking together projects exploring music.

Get notified about our first Toronto event!

Why?

Monthly Music Hackathon has been a very popular event in NYC for the past few years. Toronto needs something like this!

When & Where?

Toronto, Ontario
Exact date and location to be announced. Probably on a Saturday. Connect with us to get notified!

What's the format?

It'll be a one day event on a Saturday, from around noon till evening.

Photo of a past NYC edition of Monthly Music Hackathon

There will be workshops for attendees that are there to get their feet wet for the first time, and open areas for veterans who want to get going on their project ideas. Don't be shy - everyone is welcome!

Who can join?

Anyone who loves to tinker with music.

Is there a price?

It's FREE!

Just bring your whatever equipment you like to tinker with, e.g. your laptop, headphones, midi keyboard, soldering iron, etc... Or, if you don't have any of that stuff, just bring your smile :)

How can I help?

We're a small group of music lovers volunteering to make this event a reality. If you'd like to help out, or just wanna chat, don't hesitate to give us a shout!
